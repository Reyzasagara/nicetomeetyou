1. Electrical Design Engineer – ICE 3W Vehicle

Period: Dec 2021 – Early 2022

Skill Area: Electrical Engineering | Vehicle Systems Analysis

Role / Context:
Electrical Design Engineer for existing ICE 3W cargo vehicle platform

Problem / Condition (Before):

Electrical system knowledge was undocumented and tribal

Aftersales and production troubleshooting relied on trial-and-error

No validated electrical parameters per component

Action Taken (What You Did):

Performed full reverse engineering of vehicle wiring harness

Measured and validated voltage, current, and load behavior for each electrical component

Mapped combustion system, ignition, and accessory electrical working principles

Built internal electrical reference data for the platform

Tools / Methods Used:
Multimeter, clamp meter, wiring diagrams, physical teardown, field testing

Output / Deliverables:

Electrical system map and parameter database

Component-level electrical behavior documentation

Impact / Result (After):

Electrical issues could be diagnosed systematically instead of experimentally

Engineering team gained foundational electrical knowledge of the product

Quantitative Metrics (Estimated):

Troubleshooting time reduced ~40–50%

Electrical issue diagnosis accuracy improved significantly

Business Value:
Reduced dependency on external vendors and accelerated internal capability building

Stakeholders:
Engineering, Production, Aftersales
